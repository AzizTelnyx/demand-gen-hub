# Telnyx Brand Assets

This directory contains all the brand assets the AI creative generator needs to produce on-brand creatives.

## Quick Start

1. **Read:** `BRAND-DOCUMENTATION.md` - Fill out the brand standards
2. **Upload:** Examples of your best creatives to `examples/[platform]/`
3. **Add:** Logos to `logos/`
4. **Add:** Product screenshots to `product-visuals/` (if you have them)

## Most Important: Upload Your Best Creatives

The AI learns your style by analyzing your approved creatives.

**Upload 5-10 examples minimum:**
- 2-3 LinkedIn ads you're proud of
- 2-3 StackAdapt native ads
- 2-3 Display ads
- 1-2 Reddit ads (if you have them)

**Name them clearly:**
- Good: `healthcare-trust-linkedin-APPROVED.png`
- Bad: `IMG_1234.png`

## What Happens Next

Once you fill this out:

1. AI analyzes your examples
2. AI extracts patterns (colors, fonts, layouts, style)
3. AI generates new creatives matching YOUR style
4. You review and approve (or provide feedback to refine)
