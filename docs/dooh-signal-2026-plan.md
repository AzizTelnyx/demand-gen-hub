# Telnyx DOOH Campaign Plan — Twilio SIGNAL Conquest
**Event:** Twilio SIGNAL, May 6–7 2026, Moscone Center, San Francisco  
**Campaign Period:** April 27 – June 14, 2026 (7 weeks)  
**Total DOOH Budget:** $35,000 ($30K DOOH + $5K retargeting)  
**Created:** 2026-04-20

---

## 1. Strategy Overview

**Goal:** Dominate the SF digital out-of-home landscape around Twilio SIGNAL. Every developer, engineering leader, and tech executive attending the event or working in SF should see Telnyx messaging during the week of May 4–8.

**Approach:** 3-phase budget concentration — ramp up before the event, dominate during, sustain after.

### Budget Phases

| Phase | Dates | Duration | Budget | % of Total | Rationale |
|-------|-------|----------|--------|------------|-----------|
| **Ramp** | Apr 27 – May 4 | 8 days | $5,000 | 14% | Build awareness, seed retargeting pool |
| **Domination** | May 5 – May 9 | 5 days | $18,000 | 51% | Maximum frequency during SIGNAL week |
| **Sustain** | May 10 – Jun 14 | 36 days | $12,000 | 35% | Retargeting + continued presence |
