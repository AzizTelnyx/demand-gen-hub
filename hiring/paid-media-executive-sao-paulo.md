# Paid Media Executive, São Paulo, Brazil

**Department:** Marketing, Demand Generation

**Location:** São Paulo, Brazil (Hybrid)
**Type:** Full-time

## About Telnyx

Telnyx is the AI-Native Network for enterprises building real-time voice AI. We own and operate a global private telecom network: carrier infrastructure, telephony stack, mobile core, and edge compute. Our focus is making voice AI reliable, fast, and scalable for enterprises deploying AI agents in production.

## The Role

We're looking for an experienced Paid Media Executive who knows how to run campaigns end-to-end and is already using AI tools to work faster, smarter, and at higher volume. This role is equal parts campaign management and AI-powered execution.

You'll manage, launch, and optimize campaigns across **Google Ads, LinkedIn Ads, StackAdapt, and Reddit Ads**. You'll create ad copy and visual creative using AI tools and Canva. You'll monitor performance, optimize targeting, adjust bids, and scale what works. And you'll do it all within an AI-augmented environment where agents surface insights, flag issues, and handle routine optimization, so you can focus on the work that moves the needle.

This role bridges EMEA and the Americas. You'll own LATAM campaigns directly (localizing for Brazil and the broader region) while extending paid media coverage into North American afternoon hours.

## What You'll Do

**Campaign Management & Optimization**
- Manage paid campaigns across Google Ads (Search, Display, YouTube), LinkedIn Ads, StackAdapt (programmatic, native), and Reddit Ads
- Launch new campaigns end-to-end: audience research, targeting, ad group structure, creative, UTM tracking, landing page alignment
- Optimize running campaigns daily: bid adjustments, audience refinement, budget pacing, creative rotation, search term review
- A/B test ad copy, creative, audiences, and landing pages systematically
- Scale winning campaigns and pause underperformers based on data

**AI-Powered Ad Creation**
- Write and iterate on ad copy using AI tools, ensuring every variation is on-brand, targeted, and platform-compliant
- Build display ads, social graphics, and native ad formats in Canva and AI design tools
- Use AI for audience research, competitive analysis, and creative ideation
- Produce high volumes of quality creative across platforms and regions without sacrificing brand accuracy

**Performance & Reporting**
- Monitor campaign performance daily and take action on underperformers
- Analyze search terms, audience segments, and creative effectiveness across all four platforms
- Report on performance tied to pipeline impact: SQOs and revenue, not vanity metrics
- Track and communicate pacing, spend efficiency, and ROI by platform and campaign

**AI-Augmented Workflow**
- Work within an environment where AI agents handle routine optimizations (negative keywords, bid management, quality audits)
- Review and approve agent-generated recommendations via a custom dashboard
- Use AI tools daily as your execution multiplier: copy generation, creative production, research, analysis
- Stay current on new AI marketing tools and bring ideas for improving workflows

**LATAM Market Execution**
- Localize campaigns for Brazil and broader LATAM markets in Portuguese and Spanish
- Adapt messaging, creative, and targeting for regional audiences and buying behavior
- Build relationships with regional platform reps (Google, LinkedIn, etc.)
- Identify LATAM-specific channels and advertising opportunities
