## Demo Video Ideas

### Twitter (short, punchy, 30-60s)
- Screen record yourself calling 301-MYCLAWD from your phone, show the real-time conversation
- Split screen: your phone dialing on one side, the agent dashboard/logs on the other
- Record a call where the agent executes a tool mid-conversation (books something, pulls data, triggers a workflow)
- "I gave my AI agent a phone number. Here's what happened." hook with the live call as the payoff

### YouTube (longer, walkthrough, 3-10 min)
- Full dashboard walkthrough: setup, connect agent, make a live call, show the logs after
- Build a mini project: "I built an AI receptionist in 10 minutes with Clawdbot + ClawdTalk"
- Call your agent with a real scenario (customer support, appointment booking, order status) and show it handling the full conversation
- Compare a text-only agent vs the same agent with ClawdTalk voice, show the difference
- Show the call logs and transcripts after a demo call to prove it actually works

**Key tip:** Real calls on camera beat stock footage every time. The product sells itself when people hear it work.
