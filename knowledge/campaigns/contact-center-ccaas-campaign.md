## Positioning

For teams building contact center platforms and moving from on-prem hardware to cloud, Telnyx is the AI-ready voice infrastructure that lets you **design and own** your contact center solution rather than rent a black-box CCaaS.

Telnyx gives you Voice API, embedded Voice AI, and global numbers on a private network so you can design the contact center experience, integrate it deeply into your workflows, and scale it with predictable economics.

## 5 Messaging Pillars

### Pillar 1: Design the Experience, Don't Configure It

**Pain:** Forced into pre-scripted workflows, vendor UI constraints, "we can't do that" conversations

**Telnyx Enables:**
- Build routing, IVR, and agent logic in code using real-time call events
- Contact center adapts to context, data, and customer intent
- Differentiate your service as deeply as you need

**Outcome:** Faster iteration, deeper customization, fewer limitations

**Differentiator:** Unlike TwiML (pre-scripted) or Amazon Connect (rigid builders), Telnyx lets you write call logic that adapts instantly to live events. And unlike AI-first tools optimized for greenfield, Telnyx lets you add AI as an augmentation layer.

---

### Pillar 2: Move from On-Prem to Cloud Without Breaking Operations

**Pain:** Risk of disrupting live operations during modernization

**Telnyx Enables:**
- Keep existing voice traffic running while shifting logic to cloud
- Replace physical hardware and manual carrier processes with APIs on your timeline
- Keep existing CRM/ticketing, WFM, QA workflows intact

**Outcome:** Lower risk, lower operational burden, cleaner architecture

**Differentiator:** SIP and WebRTC run side by side on the same platform, allowing incremental migration without rip-and-replace.

---

### Pillar 3: Add AI-Powered Customer Experiences

**Pain:** AI adoption blocked by latency, integration complexity, handoff failures

**Telnyx Enables:**
- Embed AI agents directly into live call flows
- Low-latency audio and real-time streaming for human-like AI conversations
- Easy escalation to human agents
- Pre-built integrations (Salesforce, Zendesk, HubSpot, Intercom, Jira, Teams)

**Outcome:** Higher containment, better CX, fewer frustrated handoffs

**Differentiator:** AI GPUs co-located with telephony PoPs (including EU and ANZ) for lowest latency.

---

### Pillar 4: Scale Globally with Consistent Quality

**Pain:** Stitching together carriers and vendors for global expansion, quality inconsistency

**Telnyx Enables:**
- Local numbers in 140+ countries
- PSTN replacement in 60+ countries
- Private, multi-cloud global IP backbone
- Optimum routing, carrier-grade reliability

**Outcome:** Faster expansion, fewer quality issues, predictable performance

**Differentiator:** Telnyx controls the network path end-to-end, critical for global and AI-heavy workloads.

---

### Pillar 5: Protect and Grow Your Margins

**Pain:** Per-seat pricing, unpredictable costs, margin erosion at scale

**Telnyx Enables:**
- Pay-as-you-go pricing
- Costs scale with usage, not headcount
- Transparent unit economics

**Outcome:** Costs scale with usage, not headcount. Margins protected.

**Differentiator:** Competitive pricing without per-seat tax.

## Key Features

- **Private global backbone:** Multi-cloud IP network, off public internet
- **SIP and WebRTC:** Same platform, shared call control
- **Global number management:** 140+ countries, API-driven
- **HD voice quality:** Modern codecs, intelligent routing
- **Event-driven call control:** Webhook-based, dynamic routing, stateful IVRs
- **Developer-first tooling:** Mission Control, SDKs, webhooks, real-time events
- **Real-time media streaming:** Bi-directional audio for Agent Assist, monitoring
- **Voice AI orchestration:** Built-in STT, TTS, streaming interfaces
- **Multi-model AI choice:** Swap STT/TTS/LLM providers without re-architecting
- **Secure data storage:** Prompts, recordings, transcripts
- **Compliance:** SOC 2 Type II, GDPR-ready, HIPAA-ready, STIR/SHAKEN
