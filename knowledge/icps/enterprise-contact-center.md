# ICP: Enterprise Contact Center

## Overview
Large organizations with dedicated contact center operations looking to modernize with AI.

## Firmographics
- **Company Size:** 500+ employees
- **Contact Center Size:** 50+ agents
- **Industries:** Insurance, Healthcare, Banking/Financial Services, Retail, Telecom, Travel
- **Geography:** Global, primarily US, UK, EU

## Pain Points

### Operational
- High agent turnover (30-50% annually)
- Rising labor costs
- Long wait times (target <60 seconds, often 5+ minutes)
- Inconsistent service quality
- Difficulty scaling for peak periods

### Technology
- Legacy IVR systems frustrating customers
- Siloed systems and data
- Limited self-service options
- Poor routing and queue management
- Lack of real-time insights

### Business
- CSAT/NPS declining
- High cost-per-interaction ($6-12 vs $0.25 for AI)
- Compliance and security concerns
- Pressure to do more with less

## Buying Triggers

- Contract renewal with existing vendor
- Major CSAT drop or customer complaint surge
- Cost reduction mandate from leadership
- Competitor implementing AI successfully
- New CX/Contact Center leader with modernization mandate
- Merger/acquisition requiring consolidation

## Evaluation Criteria

### Must-Have
- 99.99% uptime SLA
- SOC 2 Type II, HIPAA (healthcare), PCI-DSS (financial)
- Integration with existing CRM (Salesforce, ServiceNow)
- Real-time transcription and analytics
- Multi-language support

### Nice-to-Have
- Natural language understanding
- Sentiment analysis
- Agent assist features
- Omnichannel (voice + chat + email)
- Custom voice/persona
