# Q3 2025: Contact Center — BPO Evolution & Voice AI Infrastructure Framework

## Internal Guidance (How to Use This Framework)

This framework extends the core Contact Center Messaging Framework with two additional layers:

1. **BPO Evolution** — Messaging for offshore BPOs transitioning from human-agent models to AI-augmented operations, driven by FCC regulatory pressure on offshore customer service.
2. **STT/TTS Infrastructure** — Messaging for the speech layer that underpins Voice AI adoption, targeting builders and operators who need model flexibility, low latency, and regional compliance.

These are complementary angles within the same campaign umbrella. Use BPO Evolution messaging when speaking to BPO leadership and operators. Use STT/TTS messaging when speaking to technical buyers evaluating speech infrastructure.

Each pain point is expressed in two ways:
1. **The technical reality**
2. **The human or business impact ("Net feeling")**

Use the technical framing with engineers and architects and the net feeling with executives, operators, and buyers.

# PART 1: BPO EVOLUTION

## Value Proposition

**Voice AI infrastructure that enables BPOs to transition from human-agent delivery to AI-augmented contact center operations — preserving enterprise relationships while automating call volume on a single, full-stack platform.**

---

## Elevator Pitch

The FCC is exploring rules that pressure companies to reduce offshore customer service. For BPOs in the Philippines, India, Colombia, and Mexico, this is an existential shift — their revenue model depends on offshore human labor.

Most enterprises won't reshore thousands of agents. They'll automate. The BPOs that survive will be the ones that own the AI layer instead of being replaced by it.

Telnyx provides the full infrastructure stack — Carrier → STT → LLM → TTS → Carrier — on one network with low latency and in-region processing. BPOs can build AI voice operations without building telecom infrastructure.

---

## Brand Narrative (Short)

BPOs will not disappear. They will evolve into AI-augmented service providers.

Instead of one human agent per call, the future is: AI agents handle 70–90% of volume, human agents handle escalation. BPOs keep the enterprise relationship and manage the AI layer.

The winners in this transition won't be the companies replacing BPOs — they'll be the companies enabling BPOs to evolve. Those operators control enormous enterprise relationships. If they standardize on a platform, that platform becomes the backbone of the next-generation contact center stack.

Telnyx is that platform.

### Why Telnyx

- Event-driven architecture allows incremental logic changes
- No rip-and-replace — overlay AI onto existing call flows
- API-driven number and routing management

## Pillar 1: Better Performance

### Headline
Ultra-low latency, by design

### Technical Reality
- Voice AI latency breaks conversation flow and user trust
- Audio hopping across the public internet adds unpredictable delay
- Narrowband processing degrades AI accuracy

### Net Feeling
> "Our voice AI feels laggy and robotic — every millisecond of delay breaks the conversation."

### What Telnyx Enables
- Sub-200ms round-trip latency when co-located in-region
- Voice data stays on-network, not hopping across the internet
- Same infrastructure powering Telnyx Voice AI Agents

### Why Telnyx
- H200 GPUs co-located with global telephony PoPs (Paris, Sydney, US)
- Private backbone — not public internet
- HD voice and noise suppression for cleaner AI input

---

## Pillar 2: Pricing That Scales

### Headline
Great models at great prices — no commit roulette

### Technical Reality
- Teams pay retail rates for TTS/STT and are locked into one vendor's pricing
- Switching providers means re-architecting integrations
- AI usage introduces unpredictable spend at scale

### Net Feeling
> "We're paying retail for TTS/STT and locked into one vendor's pricing. Switching is painful."

### What Telnyx Enables
- Pre-negotiated rates across leading providers (Deepgram, Rime, ElevenLabs, ResembleAI, Azure, AWS Polly, open-source)
- Volume discounts across TTS, STT, and Voice AI stack
- No long-term model commits required
- One invoice, transparent usage-based billing

### Why Telnyx
- Direct relationships with multiple STT/TTS providers
- Consolidation discounts as you add more services
- Lower-cost STT/TTS options available for cost-sensitive workloads

## Pillar 3: In-Region Compute

### Headline

### Net Feeling
> "We need voice AI for European customers but can't send audio to US servers. And non-English accuracy is terrible."

### What Telnyx Enables
- Run TTS and STT inference in-region (EU → Paris, APAC → Sydney)
- Meet GDPR, data residency, and sovereignty requirements without workarounds
- Accent-optimized models deployed where customers are

### Why Telnyx
- Regional GPU clusters: Paris, Sydney, US
- GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2 compliant infrastructure
- 100+ languages (STT), 80+ languages (TTS)
- Accent-optimized models per region

### Headline
One platform. Swap models anytime.

### Net Feeling

### Why Telnyx
