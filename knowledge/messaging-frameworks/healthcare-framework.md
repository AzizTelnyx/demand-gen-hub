# Q1 2026: Healthcare Messaging Framework

## Internal Guidance (How to Use This Framework)

This framework is designed for alignment across the GTM team when speaking to teams building contact centers.

Each pain point is expressed in two ways:
1. **The technical reality**
2. **The human or business impact ("Net feeling")**

Use the technical framing with engineers and architects and the net feeling with clinical ops, CX leaders, and executives.
