# Narrative Positioning — Quick Reference for Ad Copy

*Source: Ian's telnyx-narrative-positioning-system repo*

## The Three Pillars (every ad maps to one)

| Pillar | The Argument | The Proof |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| **Trust** | AI agents need carrier-level identity. We are the carrier. | STIR/SHAKEN A-level attestation, branded calling, AI voice detection, spam filtering |
| **Infrastructure** | One stack > stitched vendors. Ownership beats integration. | Private IP network, carrier licenses, co-located inference, single SLA |
| **Physics** | Latency is architecture, not optimization. Each vendor hop = 30-80ms. | Zero inter-provider hops. Speed of light in fiber. You can't optimize away network boundaries. |

## The Enemy: The Frankenstack

5 vendors, 5 SLAs, 5 failure points, 5 margin layers, nobody who can tell you why the call dropped at 2am.

## The Killer Question

> "When your voice AI agent goes down at 2am, who actually fixes the call?"

## Banned Words

leverage, unlock, empower, best-in-class, cutting-edge, game-changing, synergy, holistic, disrupt

## Voice

- Infrastructure company voice
- Technical precision over marketing polish
- When we're better, say it plainly with proof
- When no proof, say "[BENCHMARK PENDING]" — never fabricate

## Messaging Hierarchy

1. **Category:** Voice AI Infrastructure
2. **Core value:** AI agents require trusted communications infrastructure
3. **Platform proof:** Carrier network, co-located inference, STIR/SHAKEN
4. **Product proof:** Voice API, STT Router, TTS Router, LLM Router

**Never lead with product features. Lead with category narrative.**

## Two Messaging Modes

**Top-Down (executives, LinkedIn, category):**
Category → Value → Proof → Product

**Bottom-Up (developers, Reddit, technical):**
Problem → Solution → Architecture → Category

## Numbers Discipline

Every statistic must have:
- Verifiable source, OR
- [INTERNAL ESTIMATE], OR
- [BENCHMARK PENDING]

No implied customers. No fabricated proof points. Unverified claims are worse than no claims.
