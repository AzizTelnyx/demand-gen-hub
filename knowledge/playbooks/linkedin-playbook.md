# LinkedIn Ads Playbook

## Campaign Structure

### Naming Convention
```
YYYYMM {Funnel} {Product} LinkedIn {Geo}
```
Examples:
- `202602 TOFU Voice AI LinkedIn AMER`
- `202602 MOFU AI Agent Contact Center LinkedIn EMEA`
- `202602 ABM Enterprise LinkedIn US`

### Campaign Objectives

| Objective | Use Case | Typical CPM |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| Brand Awareness | TOFU, reach | $30-50 |
| Website Visits | MOFU, content | $40-60 |
| Engagement | Social proof | $35-55 |
| Lead Gen Forms | BOFU, leads | $50-100 |
| Website Conversions | BOFU, demos | $60-100 |

## Targeting Strategy

### Audience Layers

**Layer 1: Job Function/Title**
- Most precise targeting
- Use exact titles for BOFU, functions for TOFU
- Combine OR within layer

**Layer 2: Industry**
- Refine audience
- Use broad industries + company size for scale

**Layer 3: Company Size**
- Critical for B2B
- Segments: 1-10, 11-50, 51-200, 201-500, 501-1000, 1001-5000, 5001-10000, 10001+

**Layer 4: Geography**
- Start narrow, expand with performance
- Consider language for non-English regions

## Ad Formats

### Single Image
- Best for TOFU and brand awareness
- Specs: 1200x627 or 1200x1200
- Keep text on image <20%
- Strong headline + clear CTA

### Carousel
- Best for MOFU, telling a story
- 2-10 cards
- Each card should work standalone
- Good for features or use cases

### Video
- Best for awareness and engagement
- 15-30 seconds optimal
- Captions required (85% watch muted)
- Hook in first 3 seconds

### Document Ads
- Good for thought leadership
- PDF or PPT format
- Lead magnet potential

### Lead Gen Forms
- Highest conversion for leads
- Pre-filled with LinkedIn data
- Keep fields minimal (3-5)
- Custom questions available

### Message Ads (InMail)
- Highest response rate
- Expensive ($0.50-1.50 per send)
- Best for ABM, events
- Personal, not promotional tone

## Creative Best Practices

### Headlines (70 char)
- Lead with benefit, not feature
- Include numbers when possible
- Ask questions to drive engagement
- Avoid clickbait

### Introductory Text (150 char visible, 600 max)
- Hook in first line
- Clear value proposition
- CTA at the end
- Use line breaks for readability

### Visual Guidelines
- Use people (real, not stock if possible)
- Brand colors but not too corporate
- Contrast for mobile viewing
- Avoid cluttered designs

## ABM on LinkedIn

### Account List Upload
- CSV with company names or domains
- Match rate: 60-80% typical
- Minimum: 300 companies for audience
- Layer with titles for decision makers

### ABM Strategy
1. Upload target account list
2. Layer with relevant job titles
3. Create awareness campaign (broad)
4. Create consideration campaign (decision makers only)
5. Retarget engagers with lead gen

### ABM Creative
- Reference industry pain points
- Use case studies from similar companies
- "For [Industry] Leaders" type messaging
- Personalization where possible

## Optimization Cadence

### Daily
- Check delivery (is it spending?)
- Review lead quality (if lead gen)
- Flag disapproved ads

### Weekly
- Performance by creative
- Pause underperforming ads (<0.5% CTR)
- Audience insights review
- A/B test new creatives

### Monthly
- Full performance analysis
- Budget reallocation
- Audience expansion/refinement
- Refresh creatives (fatigue after 4-6 weeks)

## Conversion Tracking

### LinkedIn Insight Tag
- Install on all pages
- Track page views, conversions
- Build retargeting audiences

### Key Conversions
- Demo requests
- Contact form submissions
- Sign ups
- Content downloads (weighted lower)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Audience too narrow** - Need 20K+ for scale
2. **Same creative for months** - Refresh every 4-6 weeks
3. **Ignoring mobile** - 60%+ traffic is mobile
4. **Too many form fields** - Keep to 3-5
5. **Weak CTAs** - Be specific: "Download" > "Learn More"
6. **No retargeting** - Website visitors are warm
7. **Budget too low** - LinkedIn needs $50+/day to learn
