## Value Proposition

**Instant global mobile connectivity, delivered as software through a single API.**

## Elevator Pitch

Telnyx eSIM lets product teams embed global mobile connectivity directly into their apps and platforms through APIs. Activate instantly and scale worldwide across 650+ networks, while controlling pricing and experience, without carriers, contracts, or physical SIMs.

---

## Brand Narrative

eSIM does not fail because of the technology. It fails because mobile connectivity was never designed to be programmable, globally compliant, or embedded into digital products.

Telnyx rebuilt eSIM from the network up, so connectivity is delivered through APIs, activated instantly, and managed as software, not logistics. Running on Telnyx-owned infrastructure, eSIM profiles act as secure mobile identities with multi-network access across regions.

This makes global connectivity predictable, compliant, and controllable, turning eSIM from an operational dependency into a product feature that scales with modern digital businesses.
