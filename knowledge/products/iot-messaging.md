## Value Proposition

**Secure global IoT connectivity, programmable by API and reliable at scale.**

---

## Elevator Pitch

Telnyx IoT delivers secure, multi-network cellular connectivity for devices worldwide through a single API and platform. Built on Telnyx-owned infrastructure, it replaces fragmented carrier setups with one reliable, programmable network, with built-in redundancy and automatic failover to keep devices online at scale.

---

## Brand Narrative

IoT does not fail because of devices. It fails because connectivity was never designed for global scale, redundancy, or real operational control.

Telnyx rebuilt IoT connectivity from the network core up, so devices operate inside a private, software-defined cellular layer, not stitched together through roaming and resellers. This makes IoT connectivity predictable, secure, and resilient enough to support always-on, large-scale deployments across critical industries.

## Four Pillars

### Pillar 1: Global Coverage, Native by Design

**Technical Pain Points:**
- Coverage gaps from single carriers
- Regional roaming limitations
- Redesigning networks for new markets
- "We can't scale this globally without breaking connectivity."

**Telnyx Enables:**
- Multi-network IoT connectivity operating across carrier networks worldwide
- A single SIM and management model that works consistently across regions
- Automatic failover between networks

---

### Pillar 2: Embedded, Not Bolted On

**Technical Pain Points:**
- Disconnected mobile plans and voice apps
- Broken user experience and fragmented tooling
- Loss of control over device behavior
- "We can't afford IoT that lives outside our systems."

**Telnyx Enables:**
- SIM- and eSIM-based identities directly tied to enterprise policies and controls
- Unified management through a single platform

---

### Pillar 3: Programmable Control at Scale

**Technical Pain Points:**
- Manual line provisioning and management
- Regional carrier fragmentation
- Limited visibility into device activity
- "We can't manage global IoT manually."

**Telnyx Enables:**
- API-driven provisioning and lifecycle management at global scale
- Centralized control over SIMs, routing, and connectivity policies

---

### Pillar 4: Built for Reliability & Compliance

**Technical Pain Points:**
- Downtime in business-critical IoT deployments
- Regulatory uncertainty across regions
- No clear ownership of compliance and data handling
- "We can't afford compliance surprises or connectivity failures."

**Telnyx Enables:**
- Carrier-aligned, compliance-ready IoT architecture
- Support for data residency, lawful intercept, and auditability requirements
