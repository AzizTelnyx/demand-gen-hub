## Elevator Pitch

Telnyx Mobile Voice turns SIMs and eSIMs into secure, programmable enterprise voice endpoints. Calls run directly on carrier IMS / VoLTE infrastructure, not inside apps, while remaining fully integrated with Telnyx APIs and AI capabilities.

Enterprises gain global, compliant, high-quality mobile calling with real-time control, intelligence, and reliability, without consumer mobile plans, OTT apps, or fragmented voice stacks.

---

## Brand Narrative

Mobile voice is where critical enterprise communication already happens, but it was never designed for programmability, compliance, or real-time intelligence.

Most organizations rely on consumer mobile plans and app-based calling for business-critical conversations. These solutions sit outside the network, lack visibility and control, and introduce security, compliance, and quality risks.

By using the SIM or eSIM as the identity and control point, Mobile Voice operates directly on carrier IMS / VoLTE infrastructure while exposing full programmability through APIs. Calls are managed as software, governed by policy, and enhanced with real-time AI, without rerouting, recording hacks, or app dependencies.
